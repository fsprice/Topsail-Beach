Food + Drinks

Beach Shop & Grill

The Beach Shop and Grill is a casual fine dining establishment in Topsail Beach, North Carolina. They offer New-American cuisine emphasizing locally and regionally sourced organic ingredients from high-quality, small farmers and purveyors.

The Breezeway Restaurant

Informal oceanfront institution serving familiar fried & steamed seafood dishes since 1949.

Cora's Isle End Deli

From tasty sandwiches and wraps, to hearty salads and delicious soups; we're sure you'll be happy with your visit to Cora's Isle-End Deli.

Lewis Seafood

The seafood offerings at Lewis Seafood Shack are second to none. From classic fish and chips coated with a crispy batter to flavorful fish tacos, each dish is expertly prepared with the finest ingredients. Their menu also features a variety of seafood platters, including a delectable shrimp boil and a satisfying crab cake sandwich.

Scoops Microcreamery

They keep their ice cream cases stocked with 10 signature core flavors that they produce in house right on the island. There's also room for seasonal favorites and weekly specials as well as their dairy free and vegan ice cream.

Four Cousins Ice Cream

Four Cousins is a true family business where you will be treated and fed like family. You won’t find “straight off the truck” ice cream around here. Their homemade ice creams are rotated weekly and made with high-quality, fresh ingredients.
